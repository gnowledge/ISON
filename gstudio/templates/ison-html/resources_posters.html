The comet poster set

The Eyes on ISON campaign has created a set of 19 posters on comets, including how to observe comet ISON. We encourage everyone to download and print these, and display them in your school, college, or neighbourhood. These are best printed on 3x4 feet (widthxheight) with the full resolution files, and have been designed to be of good quality for flex printing too. These posters were designed by Arya Rothe, with contents from Samir Dhurde and Niruj Mohan, with support from Vigyan Prasar.

This set of posters can be freely downloaded below, for non-commercial use, by anyone. If you would like to help us translate any of these into a language you are familiar with, let us know. We will add the posters in different languages as and when we obtain them.

Click here for a (non-printable) larger gallery of all the posters.
