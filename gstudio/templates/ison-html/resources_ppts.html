Presentations

The Eyes on ISON campaign had produced and collected presentations from various people, available for download and use. A few of these have speaker notes as well. Most of these presentations are fairly long, and we expect that the user will pick a subset of slides, depending on their need. If you have a presentation of your own which you would like to share, please send it to us. Most of these presentations were made for the Eyes on ISON workshops, and we are grateful to the speakers for making these available for everybody.
