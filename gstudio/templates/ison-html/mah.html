Maharashtra Platform

We are forming an informal platform of groups and individuals who are interested in organising events around comet ISON in Maharashtra. We envisage this platform as a means to sharing resources, offering and receiving help from each other, and sharing information about each others events. If you would like to join the platform, please email Vipula Abhyankar at navnirmitilearning@gmail.com
